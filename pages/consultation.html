Services Offered

Each consultation is conducted personally. Below is the scope of guidance provided — note this is medical guidance, not emergency care or a substitute for physical examination.

Symptom Evaluation & Medical Guidance

Discuss your symptoms in detail and receive structured medical guidance on possible causes, next steps, and when to seek immediate care.

Differential Diagnosis Discussion

Explore possible diagnoses based on your symptoms and history. Note: this is not a final diagnosis.

Report Interpretation

Understand your blood tests, scan results, and discharge summaries in plain language — what they mean and what to do next.

Second Opinion on Existing Diagnosis

Receive an independent medical perspective on a diagnosis you've already received from another physician or institution.

Triage & Referral Guidance

Understand the urgency of your situation and receive clear guidance on which specialist to consult or what kind of care to seek.

Emergency Red Flag Identification

If your symptoms indicate a potential emergency, you will be clearly advised to seek immediate in-person care — this is a priority.
