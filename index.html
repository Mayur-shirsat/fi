About

A globally oriented medical professional — clinician, researcher, and founder — working across critical care, cardiovascular medicine, and health innovation.

Born in Saudi Arabia, raised in Dubai, and educated under the UK curriculum at The Winchester School, I am currently pursuing my MD at Kyrgyz State Medical Academy (KSMA) in Bishkek alongside a Fellowship in Critical Care Medicine accredited by IAOTH, UK.

My work spans clinical training, medical research, and institutional leadership — from directing the Medical Research & Publication Society at KSMA to co-founding Aurea DD, a health innovation venture. I am driven by a commitment to evidence-based medicine, rigorous research, and global clinical standards.

Long-term ambitions include PLAB, MRCP (UK), and advanced postgraduate training grounded in clinical excellence and ethical leadership.
